# Sample Story (Demo Script)

Used in the demo video

## Project Setup Fields

- **Title:** The Moon Lantern and the Whispering Forest
- **Objective:** Mira wants to return the lost Moon Lantern to the Sky Tree before dawn so her village can keep its light festival.
- **Setting:** A floating island village above a bioluminescent forest, with rope bridges, glow-moss paths, and wind spirits.

## Characters

- **Mira (11):** Curious, brave, often rushes before thinking.
- **Pip:** A tiny fox-like wind spirit who speaks in riddles.
- **Grandma Sola:** Keeper of festival stories and village songs.
- **The Hollow King:** A shadow creature that feeds on fear, not truly evil.

---

## 12-Page Plot Outline (With Intentional Gaps)

### Page 1 — Festival Night Begins

The village strings moon ribbons across the bridges. Mira is chosen to carry the Moon Lantern during the opening song.

### Page 2 — The Lantern Vanishes

A sudden gust blows out every candle. The Moon Lantern disappears from its stand, and everyone panics.

### Page 3 — The First Clue

Mira finds silver dust and fox-shaped pawprints near the old sky stairs.

### Page 4 — Enter Pip

Pip appears from a spiral of leaves and offers to guide Mira, but only if she promises to listen before she leaps.

### Page 5 — The Whispering Forest

Mira and Pip cross into glowing woods where trees repeat your loudest worries.

### Page 6 — GAP (AI Fill)

Goal for Alicia: Draft a tense scene where Mira faces an illusion tied to her fear of disappointing the village, and learns one practical lesson.

### Page 7 — Bridge of Echoes

They reach a broken bridge that rebuilds only when travelers speak the truth about what they need help with.

### Page 8 — GAP (AI Fill)

Goal for Alicia: Write a warm scene where Mira asks Pip for help for the first time, and the bridge reforms in a visually magical way.

### Page 9 — The Hollow King Appears

The Hollow King reveals he took the Lantern to keep the forest shadows from swallowing his home cave.

### Page 10 — GAP (AI Fill)

Goal for Alicia: Create a negotiation scene where Mira proposes a shared-light solution between village and cave, with clear dialogue.

### Page 11 — The Sky Tree Choice

Mira must choose: return the Lantern immediately for the festival, or pause to help stabilize light in the cave first.

### Page 12 — Ending Rule (AI Must Respect)

Alicia should write one of these endings:

1. **Complete ending:** The main conflict is resolved by page 12 with an emotionally satisfying close.
2. **Continuation hook:** The main arc is resolved enough to feel complete, but the final lines clearly hint at Chapter Two / Book Two.

No abrupt cliffhanger that ignores the page-12 boundary.

## Suggested Demo Prompts for Alicia

- "Generate page 6 in my voice, with 120-160 words, and keep it kid-friendly."
- "Rewrite page 8 to be more visual and whimsical, but keep the same plot outcome."
- "Give me two options for page 10 dialogue between Mira and the Hollow King."
- "Now finish page 12 with a strong ending and a tiny Book Two tease."

## Coaching Focus During Demo

- Keep language suitable for ages 6-12.
- Encourage concrete sensory details (light, wind, sound, texture).
- Maintain continuity from prior pages.
- Ensure page 12 either closes the story or intentionally opens the next chapter.
